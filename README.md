# Market-Analysis-PowerBi

Cloud Kitchen Opportunity Analysis: Swiggy Market Insights

### Tools & Analysis
Power BI dashboard analyzing restaurant density, price-rating correlations, geographic opportunities, and delivery time distributions.

### Project Overview

Analysis of 7,865 restaurants across Swiggy to identify profitable cloud kitchen opportunities. Analyzed competition density, pricing strategies, geographic positioning, and delivery performance to develop a market entry strategy.

#### Key Insights

Market Gaps Identified:

* Premium Asian Fusion (Korean, Japanese, Indonesian) shows low competition with high profitability potential
* ‚Çπ500-700 price point offers optimal rating-profitability balance (4.2+ ratings achievable)
* Strategic locations (Girish Park, Vardhman Premium Mall, Esplanade) have minimal competition with high ratings
* Only 15% of restaurants deliver under 40 minutes - significant speed advantage opportunity
